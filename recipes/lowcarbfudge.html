Low Carb Fudge Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 bag of sugar-free Chocolate Chips (Lily's is fine, but Kroger brand is much Cheaper)
  • 1 8 oz block of Cream Cheese
  • 1 stick butter
  • 1/3 cup Swerve Confectioners sugar
  • Optional: 2 Tablespoons sugar-free flavor syrup
  • Optional: Nuts

Steps

  1. Start by melting the chocolate chips and butter together.
  2. In a separate mixing bowl, start to whip the cream cheese and the flavor syrup if used.
  3. Add the melted chocolate and butter mixture to the whipped cream cheese and mix it for about 2 more minutes until all the ingredients are fulling combined.
  4. Mix in the Swerve confectioners sugar.
  5. Pour the fudge into a square pan and then top with nuts.
  6. Place pan in freezer for about 15 minutes to solidify.
  7. Store in refrigerator or freezer.
